Quick Tips

  • Hold Ctrl while scrolling to zoom in/out
  • Hold Alt while dragging to export content to other apps
  • Press Delete to remove selected elements (works in canvas and inspector)
  • Double-click text to edit inline
  • Right-click in canvas or inspector for context menu
  • Press Ctrl+J to join selected items into a folder
  • Use Ctrl+Shift+C to capture a region from the PDF background
